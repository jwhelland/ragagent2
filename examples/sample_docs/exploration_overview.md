# Space Exploration Overview

Space exploration is the use of astronomy and space technology to explore outer space. While the exploration of space is carried out mainly by astronomers with telescopes, its physical exploration though is conducted both by unmanned robotic space probes and human spaceflight.

## Key Players

Major organizations involved in space exploration include:

* **NASA** (National Aeronautics and Space Administration): The United States government agency responsible for the civilian space program.
* **SpaceX**: A private American aerospace manufacturer and space transport services company founded by **Elon Musk**.

## Current Targets

Current exploration efforts are primarily focused on **Mars** and the **Moon**. **Mars** is often considered the most hospitable planet in the Solar System besides Earth, making it a prime candidate for future human colonization.
